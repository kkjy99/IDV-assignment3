Inquiry of Interest

How is the population within Pasir Ris planning area changing over the years from 2000 to 2020?
What are some similarities and differences between the change in Pasir Ris compared to the whole of Singapore?


Ageing population
  • This trend can be observed at both the planning area and national level, from the constrictive pattern of the population pyramid.
  • This trend is characterized by declining birth rates (shrinking base) and longer life expectancies (widening top).
  • The trend is less pronounced at the national level, from the way the bulges of the population pyramid are less prominent .

Population spread over the years
  • The constrictive pattern of the population pyramid of Pasir Ris stays the same, while on the national level, the initial constrictive pattern has evolved and become less prominent.
  • The estimated median age changes over the years (as expected) , and while the median age group are the same in both Pasir Ris and on the national level, the estimated median age is higher on the national level.

Population datasets retrieved from: Resident Population 2000, Resident Population 2010 and Resident Population 2020.
